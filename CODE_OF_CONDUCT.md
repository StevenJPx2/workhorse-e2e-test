# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Be kind. Treat everyone with respect, patience, and empathy — whether you are reporting a bug, asking a question, or contributing code.

## Expected Behaviour

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, or have any other concerns, please open an issue on the [GitHub issue tracker](../../issues). All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
